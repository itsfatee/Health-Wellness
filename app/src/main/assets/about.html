About us

The Health Wellness community at Presentation Medical Center was opened with dedication from the Sisters of Mary of the Presentation, a healthcare system driven by those who believe they can make a difference and value nursing the sick while providing spiritual care to all who desire to know God. Our outstanding healthcare professionals provide quality, skilled healthcare services with compassionate care to meet social, psychological and spiritual needs. Our values, mission and staff actively show that our duties of healing and guidance are the core driving factors at Presentation Medical Center..
